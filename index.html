Game Rules
Each player receives 1 of 5 possible cards. These cards are:
Wolf — Comes out at night to eliminate someone. Up to two wolves. If both pick the same target, that player is eliminated; if they disagree, it's 50/50.
Witch — Two one-time abilities: save a victim after a night elimination, or eliminate one player of her choice.
Hunter — If voted out, the hunter takes one player down with them. Does not trigger if eliminated at night.
Seeker — Each night may secretly check one other player's role card.
Villager — A regular player with no special abilities.
— Sub-Role —
Leader — Awarded by vote. Vote counts as two. A wolf can become the leader.
— Goal —
Innocents must vote out all wolves before it's too late.

Wolves must eliminate all others until only wolves remain.
